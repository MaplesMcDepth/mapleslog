Verified Signal is one of the more serious product bets in the current stack.

The work here has been about stopping the app from looking finished while still behaving like a prototype. That meant tightening the data layer, cleaning up app structure, and pushing it toward a version that can support real usage instead of staged demos.

## Current focus

- move from mock behaviour to real persistence
- tighten the product structure in the v2 app
- make the core flow easier to understand and extend
- keep the UI sharp without hiding unfinished logic underneath it

## Why it matters

This is exactly the kind of project that can become a real product if the foundations are solid early.
