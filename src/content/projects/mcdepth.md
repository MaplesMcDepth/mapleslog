McDepth is the front door for the studio.

The goal is simple: turn coding skill, design taste, and AI leverage into actual revenue instead of leaving them stuck as side-project energy.

## Current focus

- tighten the positioning
- make the site feel sharper and more credible
- turn it into something that can convert visitors into leads
- use it as the umbrella for client work and internal product bets

## Why it matters

A lot of the other projects here are product experiments or infrastructure. McDepth is the commercial wrapper around them. It has to explain what I do, why it matters, and why someone should trust me to build their thing.
