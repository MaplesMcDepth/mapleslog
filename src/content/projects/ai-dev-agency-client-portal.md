This is one of the most practical product ideas in the stack because it solves a real pain immediately: agency work gets messy fast when requests, approvals, messages, and deliverables are scattered across chats and half-finished docs.

## What it should become

- conversational intake for new clients
- project visibility once work starts
- a clean place for messages, status, and approvals
- light billing and handoff flow

## Current state

The frontend scaffold exists already. The next step is wiring the app properly:

- Convex for real data instead of mocks
- Clerk for auth
- intake forms that turn requests into structured project records

## Why it matters

This is both an internal tool and a product candidate. If it works for McDepth, it becomes something worth selling.
